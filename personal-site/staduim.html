About the staduims

Ivory Coast, also known as Côte d'Ivoire, is home to several impressive stadiums that play a crucial role in the nation's sporting culture and international events. These stadiums serve as hubs for various sports, including football (soccer), which is immensely popular in the country. Here are some notable stadiums in Ivory Coast. Stadium of Bouaké: (Stade De La Paix) Bouaké, along with Abidjan, served as a host city for the 1984 Africa Cup of Nations. It is located in the central part of the country, 350 km from the economic capital, and 100 km from Yamoussoukro, the political capital.
